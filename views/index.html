About Us

Adgile Media Group is building the first ever nationwide truck side advertising network. With data at the center of all that we do, Adgile delivers advertisers the biggest bang for their advertising dollar, and state of the art audience insights to track just how big that bang is.


By leveraging last mile delivery vehicles in the most populated cities in the United States, Adgile brings OOH advertisements closer to the audience than ever before.


Our Markets


Adgile is initially launching in NYC, Chicago and Los Angeles in the summer of 2020 and then plans to quickly expand to the top 20 domestic DMAs.


Given the size of the Adgile fleet in each city, our advertising partners receive significant exposure in all geographic areas.
